// WHAT WE DO

SERVICES
WE PROVIDE

End-to-end electrical engineering services for factories, commercial complexes, infrastructure, and smart buildings — delivered with safety, precision, and code compliance.

01 🏭
Industrial Wiring & Cabling

Complete LT/HT wiring systems for factories, processing units, warehouses, and manufacturing plants. Includes cable tray systems, conduit wiring, busbar trunking, and load distribution boards.

  • XLPE/SWA armoured cable laying
  • Cable tray design and installation
  • Motor terminal connections up to 500HP
  • Industrial socket and DB board installation
  • Hazardous area (Ex-proof) wiring
02 🔋
HT/LT Substations

Design, supply, and commissioning of 11kV/33kV/66kV receiving substations, transformer yards, switchgear rooms, and complete HT cable networks per CEA/DISCOMS requirements.

  • 11kV Ring Main Units (RMU)
  • VCB / SF6 circuit breaker panels
  • Power transformer installation
  • Metering panels and CT/PT installation
  • Protection relay coordination
03 🗃️
Panel Board Fabrication

Custom-designed and fabricated MCC panels, PCC panels, APFC panels, AMF panels, synchronizing panels, and LT distribution panels built to IS:8623 standards with factory testing.

  • Motor Control Centres (MCC)
  • Power Control Centres (PCC)
  • Automatic Power Factor Correction
  • AMF & DG synchronizing panels
  • SCADA-ready smart panels
04 🤖
PLC & Industrial Automation

Complete automation solutions including PLC programming, SCADA systems, HMI integration, VFD installation, and remote monitoring for production and utility systems.

  • Siemens S7 / Allen-Bradley PLC programming
  • SCADA and remote monitoring setup
  • VFD / servo drive commissioning
  • Conveyor and process automation
  • Energy monitoring systems (EMS)
05 ☀️
Solar & Renewable Energy

Rooftop and ground-mount solar PV system design, installation, and grid integration. Net-metering applications, subsidy processing, and long-term AMC for residential and commercial clients.

  • Rooftop solar (1kW – 1MW)
  • Grid-tie and hybrid inverter systems
  • Net-metering & DGVCL coordination
  • Solar street lights and pumping
  • Battery storage (BESS) integration
06 🔥
Fire Alarm & Safety Systems

Design and installation of fire detection, alarm, and suppression systems compliant with NBC 2016 and TAC requirements. Integrated with building management systems.

  • Addressable fire alarm panels
  • Smoke, heat, and beam detectors
  • Sprinkler system pump control
  • PA / public address integration
  • NOC and fire dept. approval support
07 ⚡
Earthing & Lightning Protection

Comprehensive earthing system design including pipe earthing, plate earthing, chemical earthing, and ESE/conventional lightning arrester installation per IS:3043 and IEC 62305.

  • Chemical earthing electrodes
  • ESE lightning protection systems
  • Earth resistance testing (below 1Ω)
  • Surge protection devices (SPD)
  • Bonding and equipotential systems
08 🔍
Energy Audit & Consultancy

Independent energy audit services to identify inefficiencies, reduce power bills, improve power factor, and plan capacity upgrades — backed by certified energy auditors.

  • Detailed energy consumption analysis
  • Power factor improvement planning
  • Load flow and short-circuit studies
  • Thermography and cable testing
  • BEE certified audit reports
